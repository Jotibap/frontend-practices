Cold Coffee recipe

----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

description

Cold coffee is a refreshing, creamy drink made by blending or mixing chilled milk with coffee, sugar, and ice. The simplest version starts by dissolving instant coffee and sugar in a little cold water, then mixing it with chilled milk and pouring it over ice. For a café-style taste, you can blend the coffee, milk, sugar, and ice together to create a smooth, frothy texture, or add a scoop of vanilla ice cream for extra richness. You can also customize it with chocolate syrup, caramel, or a hint of vanilla for added flavor. Cold coffee is quick to prepare, perfect for hot days, and can be made with regular milk or dairy-free alternatives like almond or oat milk.

----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

ingredients

    • 1 cup (240 ml) chilled milk (whole milk for creaminess)
      1–2 tsp instant coffee powder (or 2 shots espresso chilled)
      1–2 tbsp sugar (adjust)
      4–6 ice cubes
      2 tbsp chilled water (if using instant coffee)
      Optional: 2 tbsp chilled whipped cream or milk froth for topping
    • ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

    instructions

    • In a small cup, mix 1–2 teaspoons instant coffee with 1–2 tablespoons sugar and add 2 tablespoons cold water. Stir until it dissolves completely.
      Take a tall glass and pour in 1 cup (240 ml) chilled milk.
      Add the coffee mixture into the milk and stir well.
      Drop in 4–6 ice cubes to chill it further.
      Optional: Top with whipped cream or a drizzle of chocolate syrup for extra flavor.
      Serve immediately and enjoy your refreshing cold coffee.
    • ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

    Cooking time and serving size information

    Preparation Time: 3–5 minutes
    Serving Size: 1 glass (250–300 ml)
    Ingredients Needed: Coffee, sugar, chilled milk, ice cubes
    Method: Dissolve coffee + sugar, mix with cold milk, add ice, serve
    Optional Add-ons: Whipped cream, chocolate syrup, or ice cream
