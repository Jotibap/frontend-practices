Bangalore - silicon city of India

Bangalore, officially known as Bengaluru, is the vibrant capital of Karnataka and one of India’s fastest-growing metropolitan cities. Popularly called the “Silicon Valley of India,” it is home to major IT companies, startups, research institutes, and a thriving tech ecosystem. The city blends modern urban culture with lush green parks, pleasant weather, rich history, and diverse food scenes, making it a hub for innovation, education, and cosmopolitan living.
